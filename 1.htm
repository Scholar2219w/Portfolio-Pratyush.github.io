It was a virtual competition where self-formed teams of 3-4 university students competed for prize money by developing solutions to an energy-based prompt. Our team delved into researching the issue, crafting a comprehensive PowerPoint, and filming a 15-minute video to explain our solution. After submitting our materials, judges meticulously reviewed our PowerPoint and video presentations.

The anticipation built as we eagerly awaited the results. To our delight, our team emerged among the top contenders, earning the opportunity to advance to the Finals. The culmination of our efforts unfolded in a live video conference format, broadcasted worldwide. It was a thrilling experience, showcasing our innovative solution and competing with other talented teams from different corners of the globe.
